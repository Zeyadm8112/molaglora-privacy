1. Information We Collect

Molaglora collects only the necessary information required to provide booking services, including full name, phone number, and appointment details.

In some cases, users may upload payment proof images (such as receipts or screenshots) for verification purposes only.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to enable appointment booking, manage clinic schedules, and improve user experience within the Molaglora platform.

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using standard security practices applied by ICE CODE.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect user data against unauthorized access or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

User data is only shared with the selected clinic to complete booking operations.

We do not share personal information outside the Molaglora system except when required by law.

5. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their data at any time by contacting support.
